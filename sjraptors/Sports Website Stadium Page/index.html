Raptor Stadium

Home of your San Jose Raptors

Raptor Stadium is the proud home of the San Jose Raptors which was built in 2010. Raptor stadium is a state of the art park that was privateley funded with over $400 million. The park features a 10 foot statue of one of the club's best players Phil Richards. In 2019 the stadium was rewarded with sports facility of the year by Sports Business Journal.


Openening Date: June 10,2010

Original Cost: $400 million

Seating Capacity: Aprrox. 60,000


Distance from Home Plate to Stands: The first row of seats are only 40 feet away from the batter.


Amenities: The Stadium has a multitude of amenities including food, beverages, luxury suites, concierge service, and conference center.
